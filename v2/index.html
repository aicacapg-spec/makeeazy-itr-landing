Frequently Asked

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Who should book an assisted filing appointment?
+
Anyone who wants their ITR filed correctly without the stress of doing it themselves. Whether you're a salaried employee, a job switcher with multiple Form 16s, an investor with capital gains, or simply someone who wants expert assurance — our assisted filing is designed for you.
Can you help if I changed jobs during the year?
+
Absolutely. Job switch cases are one of our specialties. We consolidate all your Form 16s, reconcile overlapping deductions, handle AIS/26AS mismatches, and ensure you're filing under the right ITR form and tax regime.
Can you help with capital gains from stocks and mutual funds?
+
Yes. We handle equity, mutual fund, F&O, and even crypto capital gains. Our experts compute gains/losses accurately, apply loss set-off and carry-forward rules, and file ITR-2 or ITR-3 as required with the correct schedules.
What documents do I need to share?
+
At a minimum: Form 16 (from all employers), PAN, Aadhaar, bank statements for interest income, and investment proofs (if claiming deductions). For capital gains: your broker's P&L or capital gains statement. Don't worry — after booking, our team sends you a simple checklist based on your specific case.
How long does the entire process take?
+
Most salaried and job switch filings are completed within 24-48 hours after we receive your documents. Capital gains and complex cases may take 48-72 hours. We keep you updated at every step.
Do I need to understand all the tax details before booking?
+
Not at all. That's exactly why our service exists. Just book an appointment and share your documents — our expert will handle the technical details, explain what matters in simple terms, and guide you through the process.
What if I'm not sure what kind of filing I need?
+
No problem. Book a general appointment and our expert will assess your case during the initial review. We'll recommend the right approach and give you a clear scope before proceeding — no surprises.
Can I talk to an expert before committing?
+
Yes! You can reach us on WhatsApp for a quick chat or book an appointment to speak with a tax expert directly. We're happy to answer your questions before you decide.
How is this different from self-filing on the income tax portal?
+
Self-filing requires you to understand tax forms, schedules, AIS reconciliation, and regime selection — all on your own. With MakeEazy, a qualified expert does all of this for you, ensures accuracy, maximises your refund or minimises your liability, and handles the filing end to end. It's the difference between guessing and knowing.
